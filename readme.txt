== Description ==

Pfand for WooCommerce

== Installation ==

= Default Method =
1. Go to Settings > Plugins in your administrator panel.
1. Click `Add New`
1. Search for WooCommerce Pfand
1. Click install.

= FTP Method =
1. Download the zip file.
1. Login to your `Dashboard`
1. Open your plugins bar and click `Add New`
1. Click the `upload tab`
1. Choose `woocommerce-pfand` from your downloads folder
1. Click `Install Now`
1. All done, now just activate the plugin
